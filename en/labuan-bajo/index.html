About Us

Adventures Worth
Remembering in Labuan Bajo

Labuan Bajo is the gateway to the natural wonders of Komodo National Park. Here, you can explore exotic islands, snorkel in brilliantly clear blue water, and watch Komodo dragons roam freely in their natural habitat.

With Basa Basi Trip, we bring you a sailing experience that's safe, genuinely fun, and full of moments you'll actually want to talk about. A comfortable boat and a crew that knows these waters inside out — that's how we do it.

2 Days 1 Night — Cultural Adventure

Waerebo Traditional Village

One of the most authentic experiences in all of Indonesia — a trek through the forest to the ancient village of Waerebo, famous for its cone-shaped traditional houses called Mbaru Niang.

  • Forest trekking to the traditional village
  • Overnight stay in a traditional Mbaru Niang house
  • Experience local culture and community life firsthand
  • Stunning mountain scenery across the Flores highlands
📍 Start: Labuan Bajo ⏱ 4–5 hr drive ⛰ Trek: 2–3 hrs

Nuca Molas Island

A little-known gem off the coast of Flores — white sand, turquoise water, and rolling savanna that feels like the island time forgot. Peaceful, quiet, and genuinely beautiful.

  • Pristine white sandy beach
  • Crystal-clear turquoise water
  • Unique savanna landscape
  • Calm and unspoiled — far from the tourist trail
