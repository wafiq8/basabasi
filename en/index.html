Waerebo Indigenous Village

2 Days 1 Night – Cultural Adventure

Experience the authentic soul of Flores with a trek to the ancient village of Waerebo, famed for its iconic cone-shaped traditional houses known as Mbaru Niang.

  • Jungle trekking to the indigenous highlands
  • Overnight stay in a traditional Mbaru Niang house
  • Immerse in local culture and daily traditions
  • Breathtaking panoramic mountain views
