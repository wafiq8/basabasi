in Labuan Bajo

Labuan Bajo is the gateway to the natural wonders of Komodo National Park. Here you can explore exotic islands, snorkel in crystal-clear blue waters, and witness Komodo dragons in their natural habitat.

Through Basa Basi Trip, we offer safe, exciting, and memorable sailing experiences. With comfortable boats and an experienced crew, your journey through Komodo will be a true adventure.
